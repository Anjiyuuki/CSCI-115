Ways to be Healthy

    "Healthy living" refers to sustaining a healthy lifestyle and forming healthy habits. Although it can be challenging, there are steps you can do to improve your health. Finding unhealthy behaviors and learning new, healthy ones to replace them is a crucial first step. A few examples include:

  • Eating balanced meals and nutritious foods.
  • Getting enough rest and reducing stress.
  • Practicing healthy sexual behavior, using alcohol sensibly, and refraining from drug abuse.
  • Recognizing any health risks associated with your condition and how you are being treated, engaging with your doctor to monitor these risks, and then taking appropriate action
  • Assuming responsibility for your overall health, which includes scheduling routine dental and eye exams.
